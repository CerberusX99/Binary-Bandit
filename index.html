Binary Bandit Games

We are passionate and creative video game developers, true GameMakers. We started our Bachelor of Engineering in Video Game Development in 2021, with an anticipated completion date in 2024. Proficient in Unity (both 2D and 3D), our project portfolio spans across PC, mobile, and VR platforms. Our skills cover a wide range of areas, from 3D modeling to the development of gameplay mechanics through programming. Our programming experience includes languages such as C#, C++, JavaScript (for web), and a bit of PHP.

The Matrix Exit 8

Star Wars Demo Unreal Engine 5
